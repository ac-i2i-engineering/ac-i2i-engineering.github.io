Saturday, September 21, 2024, @ 12pm, SCCE E108

STARTUPLAB

Round 1 Demo

i2i’s inaugural event, Startup Lab, challenges teams to imagine a solution that tackles an issue in our community or the world.

We wrote an algorithm to team like-minded students. Ideas find the talent they need, and people find an idea that fits their interests.

This event is an opportunity to:

  • Win i2i's resources and support
  • Create a project for your resume
  • Test an idea you have
  • Meet motivated, creative people

The Lab is designed for students with any level of knowledge and experience!

Please register by Thursday, September 19.

The top 3 ideas will receive gift cards, a spot in our 2024 cohort, and access to all i2i services (a team of developers, creatives, entrepreneurs, and an active network of alumni and faculty).

Our Engineering Team developed a matching algorithm to team students together. If you already have an idea and a team, students are welcome to register together in groups of 2-4 (every member must register for the Lab, and one member should register the team via this link by Tuesday, September 17). If you have an idea but no team, we'll match you with a team of talents, OR if you don't have an idea (yet!), we'll match you with an idea fitting your interest.

We look forward to seeing you there!
